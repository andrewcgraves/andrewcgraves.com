One of the things I like to do when I visit places is catalog my trip with at least one photo on my large format camera.

I've been taking photos since I was a kid but I have discovered I really like taking very high-quality photos to use as desktop backgrounds. I've found a sense of comfort remembering what state of mind I was in when I was taking these photos.

This is just a placeholder page until a designated tab can be created. Enjoy the photos!

PS: These are the lower quality photos to save on bandwidth.
